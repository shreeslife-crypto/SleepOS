# Sleep OS — Adaptive Sleep Operating System

**Palantir Product Design Challenge Submission**

## Overview

Sleep OS is a conceptual product design case study that reimagines sleep technology as an adaptive operating system rather than another sleep tracking application.

Instead of focusing on measuring sleep after it happens, Sleep OS continuously learns from a user's routines, work schedule, health signals, and environment to proactively protect sleep opportunity through prediction, intelligent automation, and personalized interventions.

The project explores how systems thinking, behavioral design, and adaptive intelligence can reduce decision fatigue for professionals balancing demanding work schedules with long-term health.

---

## Design Challenge

Current sleep products primarily answer:

> **"How did you sleep?"**

Sleep OS instead asks:

> **"How can we help you sleep better before problems occur?"**

The design focuses on users who already understand sleep science but struggle to consistently apply it due to competing priorities such as late meetings, cross-timezone collaboration, and everyday decision fatigue.

## Design Philosophy

This project is based on one central belief:

> **People rarely fail because they don't know how to sleep better. They fail because protecting sleep requires making difficult decisions every evening.**

Sleep OS attempts to reduce those decisions by combining observation, prediction, automation, verification, and continuous learning into a single adaptive system.
